### Pricing Rationale

**$47 one-time** is the sweet spot for several reasons:

1. **Decoy effect**: $47 is less than a service call (typical EV charger install = $500-$2,500). Compared to that, $47 is nothing.

2. **Lunch money test**: $47 = one takeout lunch for 2 people. No one argues about spending $47 on lunch. But that same lunch money buys them *permanent* priority placement.

3. **No-brainer threshold**: Below $50 is an impulse buy for most business owners. Above $100 requires thinking. $47 slides under the "do I need to think about this?" barrier.

4. **One-time > recurring**: Installers hate subscriptions (they get burned). One-time fee removes that friction entirely. "Pay once, benefit forever."

5. **Volume math**: At $47 with ~2,400 valid emails, even 3% conversion = 72 sales = $3,384. At 5% = 120 sales = $5,640. At 10% = 240 sales = $11,280.

6. **Future upsell path**: After they're in, can offer "Lead Generation Add-on" ($19/mo for automated follow-ups), "Premium Photos Package" ($27 for pro photo editing), etc.

### Psychology of the Offer

- **"Already listed"** — They're already IN the directory, so this isn't a cold pitch for something new. It's an *upgrade* to something that's already working for them. Much lower skepticism.
- **One-time** — No subscription fear. Remove the "I'll be paying forever" objection.
- **30-day guarantee** — Remove the "what if it doesn't work?" objection.
- **Stacked value** — $512 of perceived value for $47. Ratio of 10:1. Hormozi says the goal is 10x or more. We're at ~11x.
- **Scarcity** — Limit featured spots per city (e.g., 5 per city). Creates urgency.

## Phase 5: Conversion Tracking

### Set Up Simple Tracking

1. **Unique tracking links**: Each installer gets a custom link like:
   `https://ampinstall.com/upgrade?installer=slug&source=email_A`
   → This tells you exactly which variant converted which installer

2. **Reply tracking**: The CTA is "reply yes" — replies go to the Gmail inbox. Check manually or use Gmail filters to auto-label "UPGRADE INTEREST" emails.

3. **Conversion funnel to measure**:
   ```
   Email Sent  →  Opened  →  Clicked Link  →  Replied "Yes"  →  Paid $47
   ```

4. **KPI targets** (benchmarks for installer outreach):
   - Open rate: 25-35% (target)
   - Click rate: 5-8%
   - Reply rate: 3-5%
   - Conversion rate: 1.5-3%
