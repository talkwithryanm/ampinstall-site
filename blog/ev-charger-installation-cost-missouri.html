Frequently Asked Questions

How long does EV charger installation take in Missouri?

Most installations take 2-4 hours. Complex jobs (panel upgrades, long runs) take 4-8 hours.

Do I need a permit in Missouri?

Yes, most Missouri cities require a permit for hardwired EV charger installations. Your installer should handle this.

Can I install an EV charger myself in Missouri?

Missouri requires licensed electricians for hardwired installations. DIY is only possible with plug-in Level 1 chargers.
