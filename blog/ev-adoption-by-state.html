Why Density Matters

Higher installer density correlates with:

  • Lower prices: More competition means 15-25% lower installation costs
  • Faster service: Dense states average 1-2 week lead times vs 4-6 weeks in thin states
  • Higher quality: More options means homeowners can be more selective
  • Innovation: Competitive markets drive better service offerings
